_\[\*Working together with the City of São Paulo office of Human Rights, George Ferraz prepared (styled) two downtown cultural spaces for_ [_Love Positive Women_](http://jessicawhitbread.com/project/love-positive-women/) _2020 with the theme of Yemanjá and using the posters designed by Thiago. A message from the Office of Human Rights was distributed along with Valentines Day cards. The Human Rights flier encourages HIV+ women to use the city's three citizenship centers for meetings any time of the year, and provides a QR Code to find more info easily. Both_ [_Tapera Taperá_](https://taperatapera.com.br/) _bar and_ [_esponja_](http://www.esponja.info/) _spaces in São Paulo hosted Love Positive Women-related info / set-ups. Thiago made the candles for an_ [_event in NYC held on February 9th_](https://luvhurts.co/encounters/scrapbook-love-positive-women-2020-lpw2020/)_. George helped with the event too, making some more_ [_cloth hearts_](https://luvhurts.co/texts/puppy-luv-cloth-hearts/)_, like the ones distributed in_ [_São Paulo_](https://luvhurts.co/encounters/luv-game-feedback-from-sao-paulo-somos-mais-aids-walk/) _and_ [_Khartoum_](https://luvhurts.co/texts/viral-love-lpw2020/)_. There were extra hearts and we mailed them to Puerto Rico with Valentines cards. Some also went to the cast of_ [_I, OF COURSE, WAS LIVID_](https://visualaids.org/events/detail/i-of-course-was-livid-reading-talkback)_, a Love Positive Women-affiliated event at Housing Works bookstore. And, about 15 leftover candles go on to the VisualAIDS_ [_Women's Empowerment Art Therapy Workshops_](https://visualaids.org/projects/womens-empowerment-art-therapy-workshops)_. Thanks guys! xo, Todd\]_
