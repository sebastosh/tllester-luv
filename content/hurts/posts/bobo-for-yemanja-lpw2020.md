_\[\*When I asked Thiago if he'd like to make a meal in NYC--something we've done together a few times in São Paulo and once in Bamako--he said yes and immediately suggested making an offering to [Iemanjá](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yem%E1%BB%8Dja). We've worked together since 2012 (usu. [on Lanchonete.org projects](http://lanchonete.org/en/2015/10/lanchonete-org-the-10th-bamako-encounters-african-biennale-of-photography-telling-time/)), and I imagined that the idea would be good. When I heard Iemanjá and given the date of our NYC performance (Feb 9), I immediately thought of making it a [Love Positive Women 2020](https://visualaids.org/projects/love-positive-women) event. It's gonna be really cool. George saw the NYC design around the time that both [esponja](http://www.esponja.info/) and [Tapera Taperá](https://taperatapera.com.br/) (cultural spaces here in São Paulo) agreed to be styled for Amem Mulheres Positivas/[Love Positive Women 2020](https://visualaids.org/projects/love-positive-women). So then came Portuguese and Spanish versions of the same poster, pared down. If in São Paulo, drop by [esponja](http://www.esponja.info/) or [Tapera Taperá](https://taperatapera.com.br/), and in NYC, let us know if you're free on the 9th. xo todd et al\]_
