Think Twice is a collective of researchers, professionals and thinkers focusing on critical of contemporary issues in the still colonial contemporary academia and beyond. The collective works both as a [news outlet](http://thinktwicecollective.com/) and as [publishing platform](http://thinktwicecollective.com/index.php/publications/) for critical essays. To encourage the proposition of fresh ideas, Think Twice has a yearly focus on specific topics, including their own [provocations](http://thinktwicecollective.com/index.php/our-provocations/) and a yearly external call for papers.

See: [Think Twice Collective](http://thinktwicecollective.com/)
