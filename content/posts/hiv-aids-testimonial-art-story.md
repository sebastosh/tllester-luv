[Joyce McDonald](https://visualaids.org/artists/joyce-mcdonald) is an artist in many senses of the word. She is, of course, a literal artist: a talented painter and sculptor whose works often capture in stark relief the gamut of emotions she's experienced throughout her colorful life. Joyce is also a weaver of words: not just as a poet or a songwriter (she is both), but also as speaker for her church's AIDS ministry and assistant director of its children's choir. We are celebrating her for Love Positive Women 2019.
