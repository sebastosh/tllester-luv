In order to facilitate access to funding from the [Global Fund to Fight AIDS, Tuberculosis, and Malaria](https://www.theglobalfund.org/en/), the [Community Leadership and Action Collaborative (CLAC)](https://clac.cab/) created a [toolkit](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfvQajrPJm9YNpXUhF9fq6D1DSlXN7nfbUb0k7BZJVrC5dJxA/viewform) for key population-led grassroots organizations, which can be downloaded [for free online](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfvQajrPJm9YNpXUhF9fq6D1DSlXN7nfbUb0k7BZJVrC5dJxA/viewform).
