As I detail in [Relatory Bogotá](https://luvhurts.co/texts/relatory-bogota/), I got to take part in the final piece of [Luciérnagas](https://luvhurts.co/coalition/luciernagas/) with artist Daniel Santiago Salguero, offering me up-close access to a performance intervention in Bogotá's Botanical Gardens on a Friday night with thousands of people around enjoying the cool night.
